Section 2

Description of Service

Life in the UK Test 2026 is a free Android application designed to help adults prepare for the official Life in the UK test, which is administered by the UK Home Office as part of the British citizenship and settlement application process.

The App provides the following features:

  • Practice questions covering the official Life in the UK test syllabus
  • Timed mock tests that simulate the format of the official test
  • Topic-based study sessions organised by subject area
  • A daily study challenge to maintain consistent revision
  • Progress tracking, streaks, and achievement badges to encourage regular study
  • A study roadmap to guide structured preparation
  • Bookmarking and review of questions answered incorrectly
🔞 Age Requirement: The App is intended for users aged 13 and over. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you confirm that you are at least 13 years of age.
